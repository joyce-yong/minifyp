Prefix     | Role / Responsibility
g_         | Gameplay programmer
ui_        | UI programmer or designer
ld_        | Level designer
art_       | 3D/2D artist
a_         | Audio designer
so_        | ScriptableObjects / Data
vfx_       | Visual FX artist
sfx_       | Sound effects
cam_       | Camera systems
tool_      | Tooling/dev support
ext_       | External contributor / plugin

Optional Additions:
svc_       | Service systems (audio, save, etc.)
net_       | Networking
dbg_       | Debug/testing tools
anim_      | Animator/animation files
mdl_       | Models

Naming Format Examples:
g_PlayerMovement.cs
ui_MainMenu.prefab
so_EnemyStats.asset
a_Footstep.wav
mdl_Crate.fbx
